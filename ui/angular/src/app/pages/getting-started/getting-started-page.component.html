Getting started

To use the app, you first need to configure at least one chess account.

Why this app?

My Chess Opening is a lightweight companion for beginners and casual players who want to learn from their own games without drowning in indicators.

  • school Beginner-friendly: focused UI, no noise.
  • auto_awesome Simple workflow: add accounts → import → explore.
  • cloud_download Import all your games from Chess.com and Lichess (for now).
  • swap_horiz Analyze your moves (and your opponents’ replies) as White or Black.
  • filter_alt Export with filters for other tools (coming soon).
  • rocket_launch More features will be added depending on what players need.

1) Add your account(s)

Add your Chess.com and/or Lichess usernames in Chess accounts.

2) Import your games

Once accounts are configured, use Import to fetch games into the local database.
