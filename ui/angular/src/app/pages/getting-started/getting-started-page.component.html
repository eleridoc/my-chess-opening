Getting started

To use the app, you first need to configure at least one chess account.

account_circle Open chess accounts upload Go to import

1) Add your account(s)

Add your Chess.com and/or Lichess usernames in Chess accounts.

2) Import your games

Once accounts are configured, use Import to fetch games into the local database.

3) Explore your games

After the first import, you can browse Games and open the Explorer.

check_circle Accounts are configured. You can now use Dashboard / Games / Explorer.
